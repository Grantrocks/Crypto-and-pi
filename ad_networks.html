Best Ad Networks

what are the best ad networks?

1.Bitmedia

Bitmedia is one of the best ad networks I have ever seen. It has great customer support and it they have a easy to navigate site. The site approval is somewhat easy as I was accepted, and it pays a lot. So far what I have for 19 clicks is $2.26. This is most definatly the network you want to use for your ads. And if you are a advertiser this is the best site for you as your ads can only really be put on good sites by publishers.

2.Adsterra

Just wow. Im speechless. This is one of the best ad networks I have ever seen. It has by far the fastest customer support and the fastest ad loading time. Its ads always show and they will never run out of ads to display. When I had a problem in the beginning I asked for it to be fixed and it was fixed right away. This is definatly the best ad network. Use it and you will be happy.

3.Media.net

Its great customer service and its fast responding team makes it one of the best. Also this is one of the best alternatives from google. Its completely free and it has one of the best cpm rates that I have seen. I highly reccomend to sign up for this. But it is a but tough to get approved.

4.InfoLinks

This is my second most reccomended. It has a nice platform and it has a fast approval service. It has fast customer service and it has a high cpc and its ads are usually always posted.

5.Evdav

Runner up. Not the best ad network but if the others dont approve all you need to do is sign up and you are set to go. But the ads almost never show.
