Best Ad Networks

what are the best ad networks?

1. Media.net

This is by far the best ad network out there. It even beats google. Its great customer service and its fast responding team makes it the best. Also this is one of the best alternatives from google. Its completely free and it has one of the best cpm rates that I have seen. I highly reccomend to sign up for this. But it is a but tough to get approved.

InfoLinks

This is my second most reccomended. It has a nice platform and it has a fast approval service. It has fast customer service and it has a high cpc and its ads are usually always posted.

Evdav

Runner up. Not the best ad network but if the others dont approve all you need to do is sign up and you are set to go.
